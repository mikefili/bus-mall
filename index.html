Thank you for taking part in Bus Mall's focus group survey!

Each time the page loads, you will be presented with a new set of 3 products. It is then up to you to select your favorite of the three items shown, before being presented your next 3 products. Your first 15 picks will tallied and then saved. Once your results are tabulated, they will be available in a chart to the left of these instructions. You can press 'RESET' to start over and add another set of 25 choices, and so on and so forth. To completely clear all data and start from scratch, press the 'CLEAR' button.

You and your friends can take the survey as many times as you would like. The more data collected, the better we get!
